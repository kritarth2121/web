About Us

Since 2006 Ankit Sharma family business in synonomous with the art of kundan and meena jewelery. Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II invited the Ankit to jaipur to revive this once dying art form and spread its charm all over the world.

They came up with some designs and showed them to a few select Marwari families such as the Birlas and the Poddars and they liked them. Most of their celebrity clients, including the late Rajmata of Jaipur Gayatri Devi, actress Rekha, actor Dilip Kumar, visited the Ankit showroom inside the haveli.

Ankit family has been the dedicated to preserve this craft. Apart from being the pioneers in this field, the family has earned a reputation for its design and service par excellence
